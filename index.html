Figuring out cybersecurity and what each career path looks like.

Cybersecurity is the protection of internet-connected systems, including hardware, software and data, from cyberattacks. In a computing context, security comprises cybersecurity and physical security -- both are used by enterprises to protect against unauthorized access to data centers and other computerized systems. The term cybersecurity can be vague and not as helpful with someone starting a career in this field. We'll go into the categories of careers and a little about what each is responsible for.
