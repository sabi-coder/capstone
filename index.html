Figuring out information security and what each career path looks like.

Information security is the protection of internet-connected systems, including hardware, software and data, from cyber-attacks. The main objective is to protect against unauthorized access to data centers and other computerized systems. Information security can be vast and not clear for those looking into starting a career in this field. We'll go into the categories of careers and a little about what each is responsible for.
